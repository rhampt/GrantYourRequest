About Me

When the demands of life don't align well with your schedule, limitations or priorities, contact the guy who finds satisfaction in checking things off the list. I'm an energetic 55 year old interested in providing basic services that help lighten the load. My wife, a Bay graduate, and I recently moved to Bay Village after selling my retail grocery corporation. My wife works for University Hospital. I am pursuing a new career in the service arena where I can enjoy being busy, being physical and being helpful. A Bachelor Degree, 30 year marriage, four children, and owning a corporation for 26 years is what I can offer for a resume. Satisfied customers is my goal.
